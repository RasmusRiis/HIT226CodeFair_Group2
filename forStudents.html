Competitions

A range of different coding competitions with different sponsored prizes will be held during the code fair. Find what you are interrested in and sign up!

Contests

The diffent contests are listed below:

  • Business innovation challenge

    Create a prototype and or concept for solving a real life problem and then communicate this through a 5 minute presentation. Last years problem presented by the NT government was to communicate NT Road conditions using 21st century technologies.

  • Coding competitions

    Open to all students enrolled in IT courses who has created apps, programs, mobile prototypes, websites and games that demonstrate their developing knowledge and skills in building elegant, maintainable code that works
    You can enter the contest as a group or sign up with an individual project. Prizes will be awarded for different catagories of experience so polish up your project and enter the contest!

  • Poster competition

    Open to all students enrolled in IT courses who has created apps, programs, mobile prototypes, websites and games. For one of your projects, create a digital poster and be ready to present it during the code fair.
